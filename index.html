About

The Doctor
Is In

"Restoring forgotten boats one video at a time."

That Boat Doctor started with a neglected motor, a camera, and a belief that no engine is too far gone — it just needs the right hands. What started as a passion project became a community of boaters watching every teardown, diagnosis, and water test in real time.

Every engine is personally inspected, repaired, and splash-tested before it hits the market. Full history, no surprises.
